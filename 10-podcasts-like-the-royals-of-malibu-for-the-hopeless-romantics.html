    10 Podcasts like The Royals of Malibu are Imperfect Match, RomComPods, Arden, and The Archers, among others. The Royals of Malibu is a fiction romance podcast.

    The fictional podcast series is based on the popular The Royals book series by No. 1 New York Times bestselling author Erin Watt -the joint pen-name of authors Jen Fredrick and Elle Kennedy.

    It follows Ella Sinclair’s journey as she strives to remain true to herself while navigating a dramatic transformation from an underage cam girl in Fresno to a world of mansions and prep schools in Malibu.

    Season 1 of the podcast, which made its debut in 2022, delves into Book 1: Paper Princess.

    With the eagerly awaited release of Season 2 on August 28, 2023, the podcast has gained immense popularity among its listeners, piquing their interest in finding more podcasts with similar themes.

    1. Love and Noraebang

    Love and Noraebang is billed as the first romcom K-drama/telenovela podcast series. The series, which debuted in July 2022, stars Randall Park, Justin H. Min, and Francia Raisa.

    Set in modern-day Los Angeles, the podcast narrates the whirlwind romance between Jaesun (Min), the heir to a Korean Chaebol, and Ana (Raisa), a Mexican-American entrepreneur.

    After months of innocent flirting and one passionate karaoke session (referred to as noraebang in Korean), Ana learns Jaesum must return to Korea for his two-year military service.

    Will their relationship survive the long distance and everything else?

    All 10 episodes of the series are available on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts, and other audio platforms.

    2. Imperfect Match

    Imperfect Match is a 15-minute six-part podcast series presented by Meet Cute. Written and directed by Liz Fields, the series stars Arden Cho, Heather Matarazzo, Concetta Tomei, and Jake Ryan Lozano

    The podcast centers on Violet Kang (Cho), a known celebrity matchmaker tasked with finding a partner for playboy billionaire Dante Gargano (Lozano), who operates the popular dating app Cache-You.

    When Gargano crunches the analytics and discovers that his own ideal match is nowhere to be found on his dating app, he seeks Kang’s help to find his partner.

    Imperfect Match debuted on May 2, 2023. The series is available on Apple Podcasts.

    3. Jack and Lou A Gangster Love Story

    Jack and Lou A Gangster Love Story is a six-episode audio drama podcast starring American actress Lisa Kudrow as Louise Rolfe. The tale is about love in Al Capone’s Chicago.

    It is inspired by the real-life story of Rolfe (aka The Blonde Alibi) and Jack McGurn (aka Machine Gun Jack McGurn). Both were involved in the famous trial following the St. Valentine’s Day Massacre in 1929.

    The story was adapted for audio storytelling by students and recent graduates of the University of Southern California School of Cinematic Arts when the pandemic compelled the closure of USC’s in-person classes.

    It was then produced entirely remotely. The podcast series is available for listeners on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, and Google Podcasts.

    4. Margaritas & Donuts

    Margaritas & Donuts is a limited series romantic comedy podcast starring Danyella Ellett, Tavius Marshall, and LaQuita James. The six-episode podcast series is all about dating over 40.

    It follows a pediatrician named Josephine, who is pretty unlucky romantically.

    With a little push from her best friend Katrina, she starts a relationship with an ophthalmologist, Malik, who works in the office across the hall from hers.

    Malik is exactly what the pediatrician needs, but she doesn’t quite realize it. The podcast was released on October 5, 2019, and is currently available on Apple Podcasts.

    5. The Archers

    The popular British soap opera The Archers has been available as a podcast since 2007. Currently, it is broadcast on BBC Radio 4 -BBC’s main spoken-word channel.

    Broadcast since 1951, the soap was famously known as “an everyday story of country folk” and is presently marketed as “a contemporary drama in a rural setting.”

    Having aired over 20,000 episodes, The Archers is also the world’s longest-running present-day drama based on the number of episodes.

    Set in the fictional village of Ambridge in the fictional country of Borsetshire, England, the series follows the lives of the Archer family, their neighbors, friends, and other relatives.

    The Archers stars Tim Bentinck as David Archer, Felicity Finch as Ruth Archer, Louiza Patikas as Helen Archer, Charles Collingwood as Brian Aldridge, and Angela Piper as Jennifer Aldridge, among others.

    6. Spider-Man Loves Mary Jane

    Spider-Man Loves Mary Jane is a popular podcast series that has attained an impressive IMDb rating of 9.3/10. The series was released on February 14, 2022.

    Directed and written by Eddie Possehl, the podcast stars Andre King as Peter Parker, Maggie Roscher as Mary Jane Watson, and Anthony Aroya as The Watcher.

    Gavin Huffaker, Brian Detrick, Trae Gonzalez, Jason Kelsick, Miriam Spumpkin, Liam Cole, and Jackson Hoemann also star in the series.

    It tells the three-year-long love story between Peter and Mary as they are apart and together by Peter’s intricated alter-ego, Spider-Man.

    All three episodes of Spider-Man Loves Mary Jane are available on Apple Podcasts.

    7. Arden

    Arden is a fictional scripted podcast presented as a true crime story revolving around journalist Bea Casely and private detective Brenda Bentley as they solve cold cases.

    The duo also hosts a true-crime podcast. And as they spend more and more time together, their relationship grows increasingly strained.

    The series stars Michelle Agresti as Bea and Tracey Sayed as Brenda alongside Shannon Estabrook, Charlita Gaston, Benjamin Watts, and Mia Drake, among others.

    Arden Season 1 (released in September 2018) followed the pair as they worked together to solve the mysterious disappearance of a 10-year-old Hollywood starlet.

    In the second season, Bea and Brenda venture to Montana, where they get too close to the baffling mystery surrounding the unresolved killing of a rancher.

    The series is available on Apple Podcasts, PocketCasts, Stitcher, and many other fine platforms.

    8. The Wedding Proposal

    Written by John Swansiger and directed by Sara Pagluica, The Wedding Proposal is an audible original scripted, multicast romantic comedy featuring an immersive soundscape.

    It stars Scott Aiello, Rachel Boston, Audre Daly, Alexander Cendese, Jonathan Davis, Ramon De Ocampo, Stephanie Einstein, L.J. Ganser, Cary Hite, and Dina Pearlman.

    In this series, we meet Hanna, whose dream of a perfect wedding shatters when her fiance unexpectedly decides not to marry her shortly before their wedding.

    With all the wedding arrangements in place and paid for, Hanna decides to gift the wedding to a deserving couple. She enlists the assistance of Eric, the bride-to-be’s brother, to help reorganize the wedding.

    As they work together, they find themselves falling for each other. Is Hanna’s heartbreak the reason behind their attraction, or is it the start of something real and lasting?

    To find out the answer, tune in to the podcast available on Audible.

    9. RomComPods

    RomComPods is a romantic comedy podcast co-written, produced, and directed by Becca Freeman and Rachael King. The podcast has three seasons under its belt.

    Season 1 titled “Honeymoon for One” centers around Claire, a 28-year-old Chicagoan who appears to have her life perfectly sorted until the shocking discovery of her fiancé’s infidelity just a week before their wedding.

    In Season 2 of RomComPods, Vote for Love, we follow Lucy, an intern working for the Governor of Texas, who has a charming young son.

    Their first date was perfect until Lincoln suddenly vanished. Now, eight years later, her boss is running for President, and they find themselves in dire need of Lincoln’s assistance to turn the campaign around.

    In Season 3, Showmance, we meet Casey -recently jilted by her boyfriend (also her boss), she’s presented with an opportunity to launch the British spin-off of the cooking TV show she produces.

    With dreams in her heart and love in her mind, she sets off for London in search of a fresh start and romance. You can listen to RomComPods on Spotify.

    10. Once Upon a Wasteland: A Fallout Story

    Once Upon a Wasteland: A Fallout Story is a fantasy-drama romantic podcast written by Brad E. Williams. The inaugural season of the podcast was released on October 22, 2021.

    The podcast series attained two prestigious honors at the 2022 Signal Awards: the Silver Award and the Listener’s Choice Award for Best Writing.

    It follows the journey of Odessa and Elizabeth -the scribe and the spy- set against the harrowing backdrop of a post-apocalyptic wasteland in Fallout 76.

    Their love affair flourishes amidst a heart-pounding race against time as they try to solve a mystery, one that threatens not only the future of Appalachia but the remnants of civilization itself.

    It stars Letitia Lemon as Elizabeth Kirby and Vitriol Blue as Odessa Valdez alongside Lucy Middelthon, Pandora Beatrix, Aodham Scott, Maria Cheshier, Kristy Harrison, and Benjamin Campbell.

    All 26 episodes (S1 & S2) of Once Upon a Wasteland are available on Apple Podcasts and Spotify.

    Where Can I Watch Royals Of Malibu?

    Royals of Malibu is available on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and Amazon Music. You can also listen to the podcast on YouTube.

    Season 2 of the series arrived on August 28, featuring an exciting debut of three episodes on the release date. The podcast follows a weekly schedule, ensuring fresh episodes drop every Monday.

    The series currently boasts an episode count of 24.

    The latest episode (E4) titled “Just Sleep On the Floor,” was released on September 4. In this episode, Mama-Bear Brooke takes center stage, while Ella remains determined in her quest for answers.

    Stay tuned for the next episode set to launch on September 11.

    Is The Royals Of Malibu on Netflix?

    No, The Royals of Malibu is not currently available on Netflix. Moreover, there have been no announcements from Diversion Audio regarding any potential adaptation of the podcast by Netflix.

    However, the streaming giant provides a diverse selection of captivating podcast collections, featuring titles such as We Have the Receipts, The Geeked Podcast, Know It All, The Only Podcast Left, Post Play, and many others.
