    Overall Meaning

    "Come to My Window" is a lesbian love song filled with longing and desperation. Dolly Parton and Melissa Etheridge both have unique vocals that convey the emotion of the lyrics. The song speaks of a desperate desire to have somebody near, and the fear of not being able to fulfill the promises made. The energy of the song is electrifying and conveys the hope of a love longed for.


    The character in the song is in love with someone and wants them desperately, even if it means crawling through the window to be by their side. The character seems to be consumed by the love for this person and cannot sleep until they are with them. The song speaks to the desperation of the human heart and the lengths that one will go to for the person they love.
