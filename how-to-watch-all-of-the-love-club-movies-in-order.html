    The Hallmark Channel has by now become widely known for its extensive Christmas movies catalog, but that doesn’t mean the gears stop turning during the rest of the year. Perhaps because they’re so lighthearted, when in need of something easy to watch, Hallmark films are many people’s go-to, and such is the case with The Love Club movies.

    Recommended Videos

    Despite being part of a movie series, each installment can be watched as a standalone, making The Love Club a good choice whether you’re looking for a quick distraction or a full-blown movie marathon. What they all have in common, though, are themes of romance and friendship, as four women band together to help one another navigate the highs and lows of love, which may include preventing a divorce, rekindling long-lost connections, and a bit of matchmaking fun.

    As one would expect for a series about love, The Love Club films were all released during the most romantic month of the year, February, with only a week between each premiere. Directed by Jill Carter, the series has four installments, each focusing on one of the women: Nicole Everett (Brittany Bristow), Sydney Wei (Lily Gao), Lauren Bishop (Chantel Riley), and Tara Williams (Camille Stopps).

    When a movie franchise’s chronological order doesn’t match up with its release order, the viewing process can feel confusing for fans, but luckily, such a problem doesn’t exist in this case. The Love Club series is very straightforward and can be watched in order of release.

    The Love Club viewing order

    • The Love Club: Nicole’s Pen Pal
    • The Love Club: Sydney’s Journey
    • The Love Club: Lauren’s Dream
    • The Love Club: Tara’s Tune

    All four installments of The Love Club series are available for streaming on Hallmark Movies Now.
