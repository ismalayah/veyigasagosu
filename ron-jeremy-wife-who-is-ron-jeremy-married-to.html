    Ronald Jeremy is a former pornographic actor from the United States. There is no public information that shows whether Ron Jeremy was married or not.

    Ron Jeremy was ranked first in AVN’s “50 Top Porn Stars of All Time” list. Director Scott J. Gill made a documentary about him and his legacy called Porn Star: The Legend of Ron Jeremy, which was released on November 30, 2001.

    Over the years, Jeremy has been accused of sexual assault more than a dozen times. In June 2020, Jeremy was charged with four counts of rape and sexual assault involving four women.

    In August 2020, he was also charged with another 20 counts of either rape or sexual assault involving 12 women and a 15-year-old girl over 16 years from 2004 to 2020.

    Following further investigation, he was indicted on 30 sexual assault counts involving 21 victims, and he was still in jail as of December 2021, awaiting trial in May 2022.

    The Los Angeles Times reported on January 6, 2023, that Jeremy had “severe dementia” and would be declared incompetent to stand trial and placed in an indefinite psychiatric hospital.
